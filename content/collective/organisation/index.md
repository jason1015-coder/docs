# Organisation

The Nano Collective is a community-led organisation, not a company. This section covers how we coordinate, who is involved, and how the economics around contribution work.

## How we are structured

The collective is volunteer-led today, fiscally hosted by the [Open Source Collective](https://opencollective.com/nano-collective) under whose legal and financial umbrella we operate. NC has no separate legal entity of its own. Every incoming and outgoing payment received via OSC is published transparently on the Open Collective platform, in real time, visible to anyone.

A founding core team currently stewards the collective day to day. Our explicit mission is to grow the contributor community and progressively decentralise: no single entity, including us, can or should own this infrastructure long-term. Everything in this section, from how we coordinate to how funding works, is built toward the same end: giving contributors the resources and power to build AI in the open, and keeping that capability in the community's hands.

## Principles we hold ourselves to

- **Transparency by default.** Finances, governance, and decisions happen in the open. The Open Collective ledger and the Economics Charter are the two anchors.
- **No retrospective changes.** Terms that apply when you start work continue to apply to that work, even if the charter updates later.
- **Community over company.** The collective exists to serve the people building on it. Where the interests of a centralising platform and the interests of contributors diverge, we side with contributors.
